Welcome to TamedPets.com. The primary objective of TamedPets.com team is to provide Healthy pets with professional and authentic knowledge and service to pet and animal lovers.

PAYMENT AND SHIPPING

Payment details:

We are flexible in the mode of payment as per your convenience. We accept Online Transfer, NEFT, DD, Cheque or through direct deposit in the bank using our account number.

Shipping details:

These instructions are for when you purchase Pets & products from the TamedPets.com Website, all orders will be negotiated by dealing in direct.

  • We are currently shipping Pets & Accessories within India only, however if you require products sent offshore please make contact with us to discuss further.
  • We take utmost care on their travel to give hassle-free, worry-free, safe journey of your pet & accessories.
  • We transport the pets in customized "Pet-Friendly" cages and boxes to double check on their safety.
  • Our primary shipping service is Courier (for Products) and through Air (for Pets).
  • Orders are delivered according to the date we agree mutually (varies according to the mode of transportation also)
  • We do allow pickups from our place at Chennai on request.
  • The weight and dimensions of the order once packed to be dispatched determine the shipping charges. Shipping charges are collected from the clients to the actual.
